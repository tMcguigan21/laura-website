About Us



Hi welcome to Lauras Dog services, Firstly I would like to introduce a little about myself.
I'm Laura and I am 28 years old. I currently have my own dog, she is a Lurcher cross. (See the picture of her below)
She is a rescue dog, and is very energetic and full of life.

She has had no problems integrating herself into my business, and has quickly become friends with
any dog that I have looked after. I do limit the amount of dogs that I take on within any given day,
just so that your dog can receive the same amount of attention as if he/she were at home.

I am a self-employed Home-based Dog groomer, so you can rest assure that any service provided
is all monitored and cared for by myself within a friendly home environment.

I have been a qualified dog groomer for over 5 years, with certification from the City & Guild standard
at Level 2 and now 3, I have also obtained a Licence from the Chelmsford county council to be allowed to board,
as well as Dog first aid and Level one Dog training from Writtle college.
