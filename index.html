Welcome


Laura’s Dog Services is a professional Grooming, Walking and Boarding service covers the following areas in Essex:-

Witham, Braintree, Maldon, Danbury, Billericay, Stock and the surrounding areas.

The service provides a thorough and efficient dog grooming service to customers outside of their own homes for the ultimate convenience.

My home offers customer’s dogs to experience an enjoyable and relaxed environment, like as if they were at home while also having a dedicated room purely for Dog grooming which is a converted spare room, with all the facilities for all types of grooming.

Laura’s Dog Services has an experienced groomer living on-site, offering a specialised service, including time and patience for your pets needs while promoting the very best care during their treatment and stay.
